    Common Questions

    Most Popular Questions

    Can I get admission if my marks are low?

    Absolutely. We analyze your profile carefully and suggest best-fit colleges & branches that still accept your marks — through merit-based strategic mapping, not donation.

    What is the right time to start the admission process?

    January to March is ideal. The earlier you start, the more choices, better branches & lower fees you get. Late planning = limited options.

    Do you help with direct admission without donation?

    Yes — Campus Path Way follows a smart planning approach. Using alternate counselling rounds, management quota strategies & profile-based mapping, you can get admission WITHOUT donations.

    Which board students can apply – CBSE/ICSE/State Board?

    We support all boards — CBSE, ICSE, IB & all State boards. We understand each board’s marking system and accordingly align your college options.

    Can I talk to a counsellor before applying?

    Yes! Our first counselling call is FREE.
    This helps you understand your clarity level, suitable stream & real opportunities.

    What if I don’t clear the entrance exam?

    No problem — we have Plan B & Plan C strategies for every student.
    We help you get admission using alternate rounds / profile-based selection.
